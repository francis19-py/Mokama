Who We Are

The Company

Mokama Enterprise Limited is a Kenyan private limited company which was incorporated in November 2018. Since then it has been committed to delivering high quality services to its clients.

The company participates in tenders in many well-known organizations countrywide and has been pre-qualified by several organizations as well as being listed as suppliers for a wide range of mostly electricity and power field equipment.

The company has also diversified on supplies of geological, environmental and scientific equipment so as to achieve the overall objectives.
