Who We Are

The Company

Mokama Enterprise Limited is a Kenyan private limited company which was incorporated in November 2018. Since then it has been committed to delivering high quality services to its clients.

The company participates in tenders in many well-known organizations countrywide and has been pre-qualified by several organizations as well as being listed as suppliers for a wide range of mostly electricity and power field equipment.

The company has also diversified on supplies of geological, environmental and scientific equipment so as to achieve the overall objectives.

Our Services

Our Vision

To be a pre-eminent procurement, supply and manufacturing company of power related solutions.

Our Mission

To provide quality goods to the satisfaction of our customers.

Our Objective

To become a well-established supplies and trading company capable of handling Government agencies and other corporate institutions.

What We Do

Our Services

Power Equipment Supply

Supply and procurement of a wide range of electricity and power field equipment including transformers, switchgear and substations.

Manufacturing Solutions

Manufacturing of power-related solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of government agencies and corporate institutions.

Scientific Equipment

Supply of geological, environmental and scientific equipment to support research and industrial operations across Kenya.

Tender Procurement

Active participation in government and corporate tenders countrywide, with a strong track record of successful contract delivery.

Technical Consultancy

Expert consultancy in the electrical and power industry, leveraging deep technical knowledge and field experience.

Logistics & Delivery

Reliable procurement and delivery of equipment and materials to client sites across Kenya and the region.
